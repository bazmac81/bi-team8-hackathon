What is it?

Online security helps to keep your email, social media and backing accounts secure, and ensures that your information is safe online.

Why might you need to know this?

Most online services require an account. Knowing about security will help you keep your information safe.

What will you need?

Your smartphone/laptop. Optionally, you can also use a password manager.

What will you achieve

You will learn about the steps to take to keep your online life secure.

How to do it

The following tips will go through some important steps you can take to secure your accounts online, as well as additional information about extra steps you can take.

  1. Password Strength: The easiest way to keep an account secure is a strong password. This means at least 10 characters long, using letters and numbers. Also try to avoid using birthdays, initials etc.
  2. Do not re-use passwords: Each account should have its own unique password. Using the same password multiple times can lead to you losing access to everything if your password is stolen.
  3. Use a password manager: A password manager is an app that you can use to generate, and store your passwords for all of your accounts. By using a password manager you only need to know one password, and this means you can make it as complicated and strong as possible. Below you will find links to a number of popular options.
  4. Use two-factor Authentication: Two-factor Authentication is a feature that can be enabled on many services. This feature will use your phone number, or email address to send you a code each time you log on from a new location or device. You would then need to enter this code to complete your login.
  5. Dont open strange links: Always be wary of opening links in emails, or messages, when you are not completely sure what it is for. If you have any doubts, check with whoever sent it to you.
