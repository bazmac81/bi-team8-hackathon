Setting up your email

>>>>>>> nic-was/email-tutorial

What is it?

This section of our tutorial helps you to create your own email address so that you can connect with family and friends or sign up to video conferencing applications.

Why might you need to know this?

If you want to stay connected with your family and friends.

What will you need?

All you will need is your smartphone, a catchy email name and your closest contact's email addresses!

What will you achieve?

You will be able to keep in touch with your family and friends and be able to create user accounts with other applications like Zoom or Facebook.

How to do it?

  1. Click on this link to take you to GMail's create account page
  2. Follow the steps to set up your account, watch this video if you need help getting set up
  3. Download the Gmail Application from your phone's app store
  4. Log in to the Gmail Application using your new account information
  5. Send emails to your family and friends

Security Considerations

Scammers will try to gain access to your passwords and personal information by disguising emails they send to you to look like a known company or bank that you recognise. They often contain a believable story along with an instruction to click on a link that will take you to a website where you can enter your username and password.
