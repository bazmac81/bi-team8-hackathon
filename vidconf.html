Video Conferencing

As the rest of world battles to overcome the coronavirus pandemic, with social distancing becoming the new normal, people will increasingly find ways to stay connected and keep in touch with their love ones. With the advancement in technology, smartphones have a way of bringing us even closer to our family and friends by only a few touch of the screen in order to create that virtual audience.

The following our a handful of the web applications out there which gives us the video-conferencing experience provided we have them installed in our devices

  1. WhatsApp:

    WhatsApp

    It's one of the most widely used video-conferencing tool right now. As long as it's been installed on both phones of the caller and receiver. WhatsApp have tremendously cut cost in long distance communication and it only gets better as time passes

    The back-to-back encryption security on the service is a caviar, as provides the necessary protection against third party intruders

  2. Imo:

    Imo

    With just a registered phone number and/email address, you can use Imo to make video-conferencing calls to just about anyone provided they have the web application installed on their smartphones or tablets

  3. Skype:

    Skype

    As the global pandemic have forced most companies, corporations to reconsider working within the confines of office spaces, Skype has been the go-to platform for co-workers to hold meetings/share presentations

    Skype is convenient for remote work, although it could be used by private individuals who have accounts set up on their service.

  4. Zoom:

    Zoom

    This is a platform which spreads across all spectrum, be it webinar, boardroom meetings, classrooms and conferences.

    It could be used easily on phones, tablets and computers. Has particularly gained traction since working remotely became a necessity since the start of the pandemic.

    Although its usage have been mostly by professionals, it's hardly only limited to that bunch for individuals can easily make use of its video-conferencing services as well

These are a few of the easy-to-use and reliable video-conferencing web apps out there. There are many others
